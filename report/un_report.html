This report was prepared to the attention of the United Nations. It analyzes the relationship between a countrys GDP, its life expectancy and CO2 emissions. Our goal is to determine to what degree a countrys economic strength or weakness may be related to its public health status and impact on climate pollution. We hypothesize that both life expectancy and CO2 emissions increase with a countrys GDP.
